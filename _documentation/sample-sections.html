Sample sections: Bold CV/Resume Template

This document contains 4 sample sections that you can use to customize your CV/resume by adding your own sections to it.

  • 1. Section with 1 title column and 2 content columns
  • 2. Section with 3 content columns
  • 3. Section with 1 title column and 1 large (2 thirds) content column
  • 4. Section with 1 title column & 1 large column made of more rows of mixed columns

1. Section with 1 title column and 2 content columns

Below is a sample of a section with 1 title column and 2 content columns. It can be obtained by using the following code:

<div class="section">
	<div class="column titlecolumn">
		<h2 class="sectiontitle">Sample section</h2>
	</div>

	<div class="column">
		<p>Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet, consectetuer adipiscing elit, sed diam nonummy nibh euismod tincidunt ut laoreet dolore magna aliquam erat volutpat laoreet ex ea erat commodo consequat.</p>
	</div>

	<div class="column lastcolumn">				
		<p>Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet, consectetuer adipiscing elit, sed diam nonummy nibh euismod tincidunt ut laoreet dolore magna aliquam erat volutpat laoreet ex ea erat commodo consequat.</p>
	</div>

	<div class="clear"></div>
</div>

4. Section with 1 title column & 1 large column made of more rows of mixed columns

Below is a sample of a section with 1 title column and 1 large column made of more rows of mixed columns. It can be obtained by using the code below. Don't forget to use <div class="ruler"></div> to separate the rows of columns in the larger right hand container.

<div class="section">
	<div class="column titlecolumn">
		<h2 class="sectiontitle">Sample section</h2>
	</div>

	<div class="largecolumn">
		<div class="column">
			<p>Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet, consectetuer adipiscing elit, sed diam nonummy nibh euismod tincidunt ut laoreet dolore magna aliquam erat volutpat laoreet ex ea erat commodo consequat.</p>
		</div>

		<div class="column lastcolumn">				
			<p>Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet, consectetuer adipiscing elit, sed diam nonummy nibh euismod tincidunt ut laoreet dolore magna aliquam erat volutpat laoreet ex ea erat commodo consequat.</p>
		</div>

		<div class="ruler"></div>

		<p>A full width paragraph. Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet, consectetuer adipiscing elit, sed diam nonummy nibh euismod tincidunt ut laoreet dolore magna aliquam erat volutpat laoreet ex ea erat commodo consequat.</p>

		<div class="ruler"></div>

		<div class="column">
			<p>Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet, consectetuer adipiscing elit, sed diam nonummy nibh euismod tincidunt ut laoreet dolore magna aliquam erat volutpat laoreet ex ea erat commodo consequat.</p>
		</div>

		<div class="column lastcolumn">				
			<p>Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet, consectetuer adipiscing elit, sed diam nonummy nibh euismod tincidunt ut laoreet dolore magna aliquam erat volutpat laoreet ex ea erat commodo consequat.</p>
		</div>
	</div>

	<div class="clear"></div>
</div>
