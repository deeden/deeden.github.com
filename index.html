Personal & Professional Profile

I am a full-stack web engineer with many years of experience building web apps and services across a variety of platforms and languages. Today I primarily build Ruby (Rails, mostly) apps and when I'm feeling freaky, Node.js apps. I still have much to learn, but I have delivered numerous production Rails applications in my career and I am always looking for ways to get better at it.

I prefer working with start-ups building products that I or people I care about would use. I prefer companies with < 50 people, with 5-15 employees being ideal. I prefer to work with the latest version of Ruby and Rails, and will typically avoid projects on version < 3.0. I want to get better at testing and Agile development in general, so teams who are really good at this are supremely appealing to me. Mostly, I want to have fun and be productive whereever I am and be in enviroments that facilitate that.
